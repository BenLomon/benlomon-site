The other half of the equation

You don’t have an ad problem.
It’s a diversity problem.

It’s not your fault. Ever since Meta’s algorithm update, near-identical ads get grouped into one bucket.

Relaunching a winner, or running multiple versions of the same creative, does not buy you more reach.

It buys you fatigue: frequency climbs, the audience tires, your CPA creeps up... until the ad dies.

And you’re back on square one, figuring out the next ad to run.

In 2026, AI can make ads in minutes. So the ads aren’t the problem.

Judgment is. Knowing which ads to make next, and why.
